Our services

Data health checks How healthy is your organisation’s data? Do you have an orderly, well-laid-out garden, with a place for everything and easy access to what you need? Or are you struggling with a tangled, nettle-infested waste? With Noether Analytics’ data gardening expertise, you can get much closer to the first picture. Noether Analytics’ data health checks are a comprehensive examination of your organisation’s data collection, storage and application processes. From helping you clarify what data you need to collect and developing techniques for collecting cleaner, better data, to storing it in a clear, accessible way and making the best use of it that you can, a data health check can let you make better decisions both about and with your data, saving you money and improving almost every aspect of the running of your business.

Study design Have a burning question that you need answered? Not sure how to find an answer using what you have? Noether Analytics has experts in study design that will make sure that the question is answered as well as it can be, in a way that’s useful to your organisation. We’ll help you at every step of the process, from defining the questions that the study aims to answer, to collecting and analysing the appropriate data, all the way through to putting the results of the study to use.

Bespoke work Do you need our expertise, but find that none of what’s discussed above quite fits your needs? We are quite happy to take on bespoke work: contact us at iris@noether-analytics.com to arrange a meeting.
